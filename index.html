Daily Ring: Habit Tracker

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

Daily Ring: Habit Tracker ("Daily Ring," "the app," "we," "us," or "our") is a minimalist habit tracker designed to help you track habits and reminders on your device.

Information Stored by the App

Daily Ring lets you create habits, set schedules, track completions, view progress, and optionally configure reminders.

Your habit data is stored on your device. This may include:

  • Habit names
  • Habit schedules
  • Habit completion history
  • Reminder settings
  • App onboarding or tutorial state

Daily Ring does not require an account.

Data Uploading, Sharing, or Selling

Daily Ring does not upload your habit data to our servers.

Daily Ring does not sell your habit data.

Daily Ring does not share your habit data with advertisers, analytics providers, or other third parties.

Notifications

Daily Ring may ask for notification permission if you choose to enable habit reminders.

Notifications are optional. They are used to remind you about habits you have configured in the app.

You can disable reminders in the app where available, or manage notification permission through your device settings.

Ads, Analytics, and Tracking

Daily Ring does not use:

  • Advertising
  • Analytics SDKs
  • Crash reporting SDKs
  • User accounts or authentication
  • Backend sync
  • Purchases or subscriptions
  • Location tracking
  • Camera access
  • Microphone access
  • Contact access
  • Advertising ID
  • Health APIs
  • Financial data collection

Data Deletion

You can delete individual habits in the app.

Because habit data is stored on your device, you can remove local app data by uninstalling Daily Ring or by clearing the app's data through your device settings.

Daily Ring does not provide a server-side account or cloud data deletion process because habit data is not uploaded to our servers.

Children's Privacy

Daily Ring is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
